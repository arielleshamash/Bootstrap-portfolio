About Me


I recently completed a full-stack web development bootcamp through the University of Oregon in Portland. We focused mainly on Javascript and the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js), but also utilizing Git, MySQL, MongoDB, jQuery, and HTML/CSS. After dedicating six months of 40+ hours a week learning new programming languages and skills, I can confidently say I am a full-stack developer.

Currently, I work as a bartender at the Hilton in Vancouver, Washington. Although food service is a vastly different industry from technology, working as a team toward a common goal has always been an important component in my job. I also expect my communication and collaboration skills in a dynamic environment will be huge assets as a developer.
